Upcoming Events

Yearly Competition on 7 JUNE

Benefit from our guidance and support during competitive events, optimizing your performance and boosting confidence.
Dressage competitions include tests ranging in difficulty from beginning to advanced. Show jumping contests feature courses designed to challenge riders and horses of varying skill levels. Combined training events that include both dressage and show jumping stages. Gymkhana events feature timed games and obstacle courses where riders can demonstrate their speed and agility.

Camping Trip on 16 MARCH

Trail rides and expeditions organised by the horse riding club allow riders to visit stunning landscapes and natural areas on horseback.
Guided trail rides take riders through beautiful settings, allowing them to appreciate nature while bonding with their horses.
Overnight camping vacations offer trail riding during the day and camping beneath the stars at night. Exploring wildlife skills alongside horseback riding and camping broadens riders' knowledge of nature and wilderness survival strategies, increasing their outdoor experiences. These expeditions provide riders wonderful experiences in nature, establishing a stronger bond with their equine companions and creating lasting memories.

Charity Fundraiser on 24 APRIL

Charity rides are fundraisers to benefit local horse charities, rescue organisations, and community initiatives. Silent auctions, raffles, and bake sales for fundraising events used to support club activities, facility renovations, and charitable causes. Members can suggest the which charity to give the money too, to do so click on the contact page of this website.
